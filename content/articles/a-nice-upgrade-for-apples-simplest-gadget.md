## Background & Context
The original **AirTag** was released in 2021 and was designed to provide a simple and efficient way for users to track their belongings. The device used **Bluetooth Low Energy** technology to connect to the user's iPhone or iPad, allowing them to track the location of their items. However, the original AirTag had some limitations, including a relatively short range and limited findability. The new AirTag addresses these issues, providing an expanded connectivity range and improved findability. This upgrade is seen as a significant improvement over the original model, and is expected to make the AirTag a more attractive option for users.
