## Introduction
In a move to expand its presence in the high-end home audio market, **Sonos** has announced the introduction of Amp Multi, a professional-grade multi-channel streaming amplifier. This new device is designed to cater to the needs of custom installers and homeowners with complicated residential installs. With Amp Multi, **Sonos** aims to provide a solution that combines high-quality audio with flexibility and ease of use.

## Background & Context
**Sonos** has been a leading player in the home audio market for several years, known for its high-quality speakers and streaming devices. However, the company has faced increasing competition from other manufacturers, and its stock price has been affected by the rise of smart speakers and voice assistants. In recent years, **Sonos** has focused on improving its app and software capabilities, which has helped to turnaround its business. The introduction of Amp Multi marks the company's first new hardware release in over a year, and it is expected to play a significant role in **Sonos**' strategy to expand its presence in the high-end home audio market.

### What is Amp Multi?
Amp Multi is a professional-grade multi-channel streaming amplifier that is designed to provide high-quality audio and flexibility for custom installers and homeowners. The device features a compact design and is capable of streaming audio from a variety of sources, including **Sonos**' own speakers and third-party devices. Amp Multi also supports multiple audio formats, including Dolby Atmos and DTS:X, making it an ideal solution for home theaters and other high-end audio applications.
