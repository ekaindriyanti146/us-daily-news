## The Details
The growth in travel destinations is not limited to international travel. Domestic travel is also on the rise, with many Americans opting to explore their own country rather than traveling abroad. The rise of remote work has also contributed to the growth in domestic travel, with many Americans taking advantage of the flexibility to work from anywhere and combining work with leisure travel.

## Background and Context
The COVID-19 pandemic had a devastating impact on the travel industry, with many countries imposing strict travel restrictions and lockdowns. However, with the rollout of vaccination programs and the easing of travel restrictions, the industry is slowly recovering. The growth in travel destinations is also being driven by the increasing demand for experiential travel, with many travelers seeking unique and authentic experiences. The rise of social media has also contributed to the growth in travel, with many travelers sharing their experiences and inspiring others to travel.
