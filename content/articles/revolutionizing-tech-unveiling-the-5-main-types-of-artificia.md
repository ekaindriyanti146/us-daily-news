## The Details: Understanding the 5 Main Types
The five main types of artificial intelligence are Reactive Machines, Limited Memory, Theory of Mind, Self-Aware, and Superintelligence. Reactive Machines are the most basic type of AI, which can only react to currently existing situations. Limited Memory, on the other hand, can learn from historical data to make decisions. Theory of Mind AI can understand emotions and thoughts, while Self-Aware AI has its own consciousness. Superintelligence is the most advanced type, surpassing human intelligence in every domain.
