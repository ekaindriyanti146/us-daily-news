# Explosive Action Unfolds in 'White House Down'
Who: Channing Tatum, Jamie Foxx, and Maggie Gyllenhaal star in the film. 
What: The movie 'White House Down' is an action-packed thriller. 
When: Released in 2013, the film has gained a significant following over the years. 
Where: The story takes place in Washington D.C., with the White House being the central location. 
How: The film's plot revolves around a Capitol Police officer who must rescue the President from a paramilitary group.

## The Details
'White House Down' is a 2013 American action thriller film directed by Roland Emmerich and written by James Vanderbilt. The movie follows John Cale (Channing Tatum), a U.S. Capitol Police officer who is rejected for a job with the Secret Service. Cale's daughter, Emily (Joey King), is a huge fan of the President, and to impress her, he takes her on a tour of the White House. However, their tour is disrupted when a paramilitary group, led by Emil Stenz (Jason Clarke), launches a violent takeover of the White House.

As the story unfolds, Cale finds himself in a desperate bid to save his daughter and the President, James W. Sawyer (Jamie Foxx), from the clutches of the terrorists. Along the way, he teams up with the President, and together they must use their wits and whatever resources they can find to take down the enemy and save the country.

## Background/Context
'White House Down' is not the only film to feature a plot involving a takeover of the White House. In 2013, another movie, 'Olympus Has Fallen,' was released, which also features a similar storyline. However, 'White House Down' has been praised for its unique blend of action, suspense, and humor. The film's director, Roland Emmerich, is known for his work on disaster movies like 'Independence Day' and '2012,' and he brings his signature style to 'White House Down.'

## Reaction/Quotes
'White House Down' has received mixed reviews from critics, but audiences have praised the film's non-stop action and entertaining storyline. In an interview, Channing Tatum said, 'I loved the idea of playing a regular guy who finds himself in an extraordinary situation. John Cale is a hero, but he's not a superhero – he's just a guy who wants to protect his daughter and the President.'

Jamie Foxx, who plays the President, added, 'I think what sets 'White House Down' apart from other action movies is the chemistry between the characters. Channing and I had a great time working together, and I think that comes across on screen.'

Maggie Gyllenhaal, who plays a Secret Service agent, said, 'I was drawn to the film because of its strong female characters. My character, Carol Finnerty, is a tough and capable agent who plays a key role in the story.'

Overall, 'White House Down' is an action-packed thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With its talented cast, exciting storyline, and impressive action sequences, it's a must-see for fans of the genre.
