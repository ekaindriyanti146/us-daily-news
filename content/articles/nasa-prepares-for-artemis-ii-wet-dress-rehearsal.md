As part of the preparations, NASA is conducting a wet dress rehearsal, a critical test that will simulate the launch countdown and ensure that the spacecraft and launch vehicle are ready for liftoff. The rehearsal will take place at the **Kennedy Space Center** in Florida, where the **Artemis II** rocket is currently situated.
