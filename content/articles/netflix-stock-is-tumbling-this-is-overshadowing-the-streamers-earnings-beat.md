## Background & Context
Netflix has been a pioneer in the streaming industry, revolutionizing the way people consume entertainment content. With its vast library of original content, including popular shows like **Stranger Things** and **The Crown**, the company has attracted millions of subscribers worldwide. However, the streaming landscape has become increasingly competitive, with new players like **Disney+** and **HBO Max** entering the market. To stay ahead of the game, Netflix has been investing heavily in new content, including movies and TV shows.

The bidding war for Warner Bros is a significant development in the streaming industry. Warner Bros is a major player in the entertainment industry, with a vast library of content, including popular franchises like **Harry Potter** and **DC Comics**. Acquiring Warner Bros would give Netflix a significant boost in terms of content offerings, allowing it to compete more effectively with its rivals.

## Analysis
