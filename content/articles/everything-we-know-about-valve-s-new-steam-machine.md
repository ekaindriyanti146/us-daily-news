## Introduction
Valve, the renowned gaming company behind the popular Steam platform, has been working on a new console called the Steam Machine. The console is designed to bring PC gaming to the living room, with a focus on performance, affordability, and ease of use. In this article, we will delve into the latest updates on the Steam Machine, including its release date, specifications, and pricing.

## Background & Context
The Steam Machine was first announced in 2013, with Valve partnering with various hardware manufacturers to create a range of consoles that would run the Steam operating system. According to AMD, the Steam Machine will feature a custom AMD processor and will be capable of playing most games at 4K/60fps.
