The successful test brings NASA one step closer to returning astronauts to the moon by 2025, a goal set by the **NASA Administration**. The Artemis 2 mission will send the first woman and the first person of color to the moon's surface, marking a major milestone in the history of space exploration.

## Background & Context
The **Artemis 2** mission is part of NASA's larger **Artemis program**, which aims to establish a sustainable human presence on the lunar surface. ### The Wet Dress Rehearsal
The wet dress rehearsal is a critical test that involves fueling the rocket with propellant and conducting a practice countdown. The test is designed to simulate the actual launch conditions and provides an opportunity for the launch team to practice and refine their procedures. The test also allows NASA to identify and fix any issues that may arise during the launch process. During the test, the rocket was fueled with over 700,000 gallons of propellant, and the launch team conducted a practice countdown, simulating the actual launch sequence.
