## Introduction
Apple has officially announced the release of the new AirTag 2, a device designed to help users keep track of their belongings. The AirTag 2 boasts an expanded connectivity range, a louder speaker, and improved findability features. This upgrade comes after five years of customer feedback and demand for a more efficient and reliable tracking device. ## Background & Context
The original AirTag was released in 2021, and since then, it has become a popular device for tracking personal items such as keys, wallets, and luggage. However, users have consistently requested improvements to the device's range and findability. Apple has responded to these demands by releasing the AirTag 2, which features a more powerful Bluetooth connectivity range and a louder speaker. This upgrade enables users to locate their AirTag 2 more easily, even in noisy environments or when the device is out of sight.

### AirTag 2 Features
The AirTag 2 boasts several notable features, including:
* Expanded connectivity range: The AirTag 2 has a more extensive Bluetooth range, allowing users to connect to the device from a greater distance.
* Louder speaker: The AirTag 2 features a louder speaker, making it easier for users to locate the device, even in noisy environments.
* Improved findability: The AirTag 2 has enhanced precision finding capabilities, allowing users to pinpoint the device's location more accurately.
* Compatibility: The AirTag 2 is compatible with iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 16.4 or later.
