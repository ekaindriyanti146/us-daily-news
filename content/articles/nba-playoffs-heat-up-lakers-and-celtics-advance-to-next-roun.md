The Los Angeles Lakers and the Boston Celtics have advanced to the next round of the NBA playoffs, following decisive victories over their respective opponents. The Lakers defeated the Denver Nuggets 4-2 in their series, while the Celtics swept the Philadelphia 76ers 4-0.

The Lakers' win was marked by a dominant performance from LeBron James, who scored 38 points and had 16 rebounds in the final game of the series. Anthony Davis also played a crucial role, contributing 27 points and 11 rebounds. The Celtics' defense was also impressive, holding the 76ers to under 100 points in three of the four games.
