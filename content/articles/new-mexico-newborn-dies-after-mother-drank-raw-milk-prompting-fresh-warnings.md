### What is Listeria?
**Listeria** is a type of bacteria that can be found in unpasteurized dairy products, including **raw milk**. It can cause serious illness in pregnant women, older adults, and people with weakened immune systems. **Listeria** can lead to **stillbirth**, **miscarriage**, and **premature birth**, as well as **meningitis** and **sepsis** in newborns.
