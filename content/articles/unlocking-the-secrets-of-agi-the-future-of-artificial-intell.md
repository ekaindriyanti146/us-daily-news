## The Details: Understanding AGI
AGI refers to a type of artificial intelligence that possesses the ability to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a broad spectrum of tasks, similar to human intelligence. Unlike narrow or weak AI, which is designed to perform a specific task, AGI aims to create machines that can think, reason, and solve problems like humans. This technology has the potential to revolutionize industries such as healthcare, finance, and education, by providing intelligent machines that can assist and augment human capabilities.
