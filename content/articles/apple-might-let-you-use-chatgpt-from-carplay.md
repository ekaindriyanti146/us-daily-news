## Introduction
In a significant development, **Apple** is reportedly planning to allow the integration of external voice-controlled AI chatbots, including **ChatGPT**, in its **CarPlay** system. This move is expected to revolutionize the driving experience, providing users with more personalized and interactive features. As per recent reports, **Apple** is working on enabling **CarPlay** to support **ChatGPT** and other AI chatbots, developed by companies like **OpenAI** and **Google**. The announcement is expected to be made at an upcoming **Apple** event, where the company will unveil its plans to open up **CarPlay** to third-party developers.

## Background & Context
**CarPlay** is **Apple's** infotainment system, designed to provide a seamless driving experience by integrating **iPhone** features with the car's dashboard. Currently, **CarPlay** supports various apps, including music streaming services, navigation, and messaging. However, the integration of external AI chatbots like **ChatGPT** is a significant development, as it will enable drivers to interact with their cars in a more conversational and personalized way. **ChatGPT**, developed by **OpenAI**, is a popular AI chatbot known for its ability to understand and respond to natural language inputs.

### History of CarPlay
**CarPlay** was first introduced by **Apple** in 2014, with the aim of providing a safe and intuitive way for drivers to use their **iPhone** features while driving. Over the years, **CarPlay** has evolved to support various apps and features, including **Siri**, **Apple Maps**, and **Apple Music**. The integration of external AI chatbots like **ChatGPT** is the next step in the evolution of **CarPlay**, as it will enable drivers to access a wide range of information and services using conversational interfaces.
