The island is also rich in natural resources, including **iron ore**, **uranium**, and **rare earth minerals**. In recent years, **China** has shown increasing interest in **Greenland**, investing in various infrastructure projects and seeking to establish a foothold in the region.

The idea of the US taking over **Greenland** is not new, with **President Harry Truman** having attempted to purchase the island from Denmark in 1946. However, the current situation is different, with **Trump's administration** facing significant opposition from European leaders and NATO allies.
