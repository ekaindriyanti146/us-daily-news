The Crew Dragon is a reusable spacecraft designed to carry astronauts to and from the International Space Station, with the ability to carry up to seven crew members. The spacecraft is launched atop the Falcon 9 rocket, which has become a workhorse for SpaceX, with numerous successful launches under its belt. The partnership between NASA and SpaceX has been instrumental in the development of the Crew Dragon, with NASA providing funding and expertise, and SpaceX providing the hardware and launch services.

The International Space Station is a habitable artificial satellite in low Earth orbit, where astronauts and cosmonauts live and work for extended periods. The station is also a vital platform for testing and developing the technologies and strategies needed for long-duration spaceflight, such as those required for missions to the Moon and Mars.
