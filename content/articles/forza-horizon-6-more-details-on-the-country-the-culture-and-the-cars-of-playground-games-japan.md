Forza Horizon 6 is developed by Playground Games, a renowned studio known for its work on the Forza Horizon series. The game is set in Japan, a country with a unique blend of traditional and modern culture. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the scenic countryside, Japan offers a diverse range of environments for players to explore. With over 550 cars to collect and customize, players will have ample opportunities to showcase their driving skills and style.
