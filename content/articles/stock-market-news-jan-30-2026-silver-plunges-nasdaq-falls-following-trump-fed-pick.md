**Kevin Warsh**, a former member of the **Federal Reserve** Board of Governors, has been nominated by **Trump** to succeed **Jerome Powell** as **Federal Reserve** chair. **Warsh** has been a vocal critic of the **Federal Reserve**'s monetary policy, advocating for a more hawkish approach to interest rates.
