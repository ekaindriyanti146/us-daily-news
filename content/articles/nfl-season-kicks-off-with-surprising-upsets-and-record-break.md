## Background and Context
The NFL season is a highly anticipated event, with millions of fans tuning in each week to watch their favorite teams compete. The league consists of 32 teams, divided into two conferences: the American Football Conference (AFC) and the National Football Conference (NFC). Each team plays 17 games during the regular season, with the top teams in each conference advancing to the playoffs. The playoffs culminate in the Super Bowl, the championship game of the NFL.
