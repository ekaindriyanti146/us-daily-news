Injuries have also played a significant role in shaping the NBA season. The Golden State Warriors, for example, have been without Stephen Curry for several weeks due to a leg injury. Curry, a two-time NBA champion and two-time MVP, is expected to return to the court soon, but his absence has been felt. The Warriors have struggled to find consistency, and their playoff hopes are beginning to slip away.

The Chicago Bulls, meanwhile, have been dealing with injuries to key players like Zach LaVine and Lonzo Ball.
