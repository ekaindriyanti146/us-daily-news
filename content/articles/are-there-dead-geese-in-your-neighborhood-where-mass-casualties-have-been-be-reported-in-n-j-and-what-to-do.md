### What is Bird Flu?
Bird flu, also known as avian influenza, is a type of flu that affects birds. The disease is caused by a virus that can be spread through contact with infected birds, their droppings, or contaminated surfaces. While bird flu is typically not contagious to humans, it can be deadly to birds, with mortality rates reaching up to 100% in some cases.
