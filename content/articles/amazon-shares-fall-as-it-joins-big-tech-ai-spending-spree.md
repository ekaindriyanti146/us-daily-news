## Introduction
Amazon, the world's largest online retailer, has seen its shares plummet by 10% after announcing a massive $200 billion spending forecast on artificial intelligence (AI) technology. The company's earnings have also missed expectations, contributing to the decline in share price. This move is part of a larger trend in the tech industry, as companies like Google, Microsoft, and Facebook invest heavily in AI research and development. As the tech landscape continues to evolve, Amazon is joining the ranks of its competitors in the AI spending spree. But what does this mean for the company, its investors, and the broader tech industry?
