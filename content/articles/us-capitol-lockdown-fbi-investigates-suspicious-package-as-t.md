# Breaking News: US Capitol Placed on Lockdown
The US Capitol building in Washington D.C. was placed on lockdown earlier today due to a suspicious package found on the premises. According to reports, the package was discovered at approximately 9:45 am EST, prompting an immediate response from law enforcement and Capitol security.

## The Details
The FBI is currently investigating the package, which was found in a Senate office building. Capitol Police have evacuated the area and are conducting a thorough search of the building. The lockdown affects not only the Capitol building but also the surrounding offices and streets.
