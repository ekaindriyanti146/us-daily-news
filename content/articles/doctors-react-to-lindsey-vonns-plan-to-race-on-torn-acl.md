### The Injury
A torn ACL, or anterior cruciate ligament, is a serious injury that can have significant implications for an athlete's career. The ACL is a critical ligament that provides stability to the knee joint, and a tear can cause pain, swelling, and limited mobility. In Vonn's case, the injury is particularly concerning, as it could affect her ability to perform the high-speed turns and jumps required in downhill skiing. According to [Dr. Robert LaPrade](/articles/dr-robert-laprade-orthopedic-surgeon), a leading expert in sports medicine, "A torn ACL is a significant injury that requires careful management and rehabilitation.
