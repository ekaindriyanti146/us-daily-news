In a shocking move, **Donald Trump**, the 45th President of the United States, has filed a lawsuit against **JPMorgan Chase**, one of the largest banks in the world, and its CEO **Jamie Dimon**. This move has sparked a heated debate about the role of politics in business and the potential consequences of "debanking" individuals or companies.

The bank cited concerns about Trump's involvement in the incident and the potential reputational risk associated with continuing to do business with him. However, Trump claims that the bank's actions were a result of political pressure and that the decision has caused him significant financial harm.
