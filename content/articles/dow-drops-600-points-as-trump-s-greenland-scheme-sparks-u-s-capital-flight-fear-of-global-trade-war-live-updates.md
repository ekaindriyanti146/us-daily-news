### Dow Drops 600 Points as Trump's Greenland Scheme Sparks US Capital Flight, Fear of Global Trade War: Live Updates
The Dow Jones Industrial Average plummeted more than 600 points on Monday, with the US dollar and Treasury prices tumbling, as investors feared a potential global trade war sparked by President Trump's Greenland scheme and tariff threats.

#### Trump's Trade Rep Says Greenland Tariff Threat Could 'Set the Scene' for Negotiation
According to a report by Politico, Trump's trade representative stated that the tariff threat on Greenland could be a negotiating tactic to 'set the scene' for future trade talks.
