## Background and Context
Ethereum was created in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin, a Russian-Canadian programmer, with the goal of creating a decentralized platform for building smart contracts and decentralized applications. Since its launch, Ethereum has grown to become one of the largest and most widely-used cryptocurrencies in the world, with a market capitalization of over $500 billion.

The upcoming upgrade to Ethereum 2.0, which is expected to take place later this year, is also expected to have a positive impact on the price of Ethereum. Ethereum 2.0 will introduce a number of significant changes to the Ethereum network, including the transition from a proof-of-work consensus algorithm to a proof-of-stake algorithm, which is expected to improve the network's scalability and security.
