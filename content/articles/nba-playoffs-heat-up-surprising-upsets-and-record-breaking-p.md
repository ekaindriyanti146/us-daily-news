## Background and Context of the NBA Playoffs
The NBA playoffs are a single-elimination tournament held at the end of the regular season. The top eight teams from each conference qualify for the playoffs, with the teams seeded based on their win-loss record. The playoffs consist of four rounds: the first round, the conference semifinals, the conference finals, and the NBA Finals. The team that wins the most games in each series advances to the next round, with the ultimate goal of being crowned the NBA champion.
