## Introduction
The Walt **Disney** Company has announced that **Josh D'Amaro** will take over as the new CEO, replacing **Bob Iger**. This change in leadership has sparked interest and speculation about what the future holds for the media giant. As D'Amaro steps into his new role, he will be responsible for guiding the company's overall strategy and direction. With a background in theme parks and resorts, D'Amaro brings a unique perspective to the role. But what can we expect from the new CEO, and how will his leadership impact the company's various divisions, including its movie studio side?

## Background & Context
**Josh D'Amaro** has been with **Disney** for over 20 years, working his way up through the ranks to become the chairman of Disney Parks, Experiences and Products. During his tenure, he has overseen the successful expansion of the company's theme park and resort business, including the opening of new parks and attractions. D'Amaro's experience in this area is expected to bring a fresh perspective to the CEO role, and his commitment to innovation and customer experience is likely to shape the company's future direction.

The change in leadership comes at a time of significant change and challenge for the media industry. The rise of streaming services has disrupted traditional business models, and companies like **Disney** must adapt to remain competitive. **Bob Iger**'s retirement marks the end of an era for **Disney**, and D'Amaro's appointment represents a new chapter in the company's history.

## Analysis
The appointment of **Josh D'Amaro** as CEO is seen as a sign of continuity for **Disney**'s movie studio side.
