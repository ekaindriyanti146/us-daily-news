# Walmart Enhances Store Locator Feature
As the phrase 'Walmart Near Me' continues to trend, Walmart has revamped its store locator feature to provide users with a more seamless and intuitive experience. The updated feature, available on the company's website and mobile app, allows customers to quickly find their nearest Walmart store, complete with directions, store hours, and available services.

## The Details
The revamped store locator feature includes several key enhancements, such as improved search functionality, enhanced map views, and more detailed store information. Customers can now search for stores by city, state, or zip code, and even filter results by services offered, such as pharmacy, optical, or grocery pickup. The feature also includes a 'store details' page, which provides information on store hours, contact details, and available amenities.
