## Introduction
The Crew-12 mission, a collaboration between **NASA** and **SpaceX**, launched successfully from Florida on February 13. The mission is an important step in the ongoing research and development of space exploration, and will see the crew conduct various research experiments and tech tests during their time in space. The launch was delayed due to technical issues, but the **FAA** cleared the **Falcon 9** rocket to fly again, allowing the mission to proceed as planned. The crew will spend several months in space, conducting research and testing new technologies, before returning to Earth.
