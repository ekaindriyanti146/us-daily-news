## Introduction
A devastating 100-vehicle pileup occurred on I-196 in Michigan, leaving **9 people injured** and highlighting the vulnerabilities of US infrastructure in the face of severe weather conditions. The incident happened as a powerful snowstorm moved across the country, bringing heavy snowfall and freezing temperatures to several states. The pileup is a stark reminder of the importance of investing in **infrastructure** and **emergency response** systems to mitigate the impact of such events.
